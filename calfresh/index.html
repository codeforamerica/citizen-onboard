CalFresh

CalFresh is California's Food Stamps program. Residents in 18 counties can apply for CalFresh online through My Benefits CalWin. Let's see how it goes...

Stats

  • ~50 screens
  • ~200 questions
  • ~2 errors (started over once)
  • ~2 hours to apply
  • ~2 weeks until determination
